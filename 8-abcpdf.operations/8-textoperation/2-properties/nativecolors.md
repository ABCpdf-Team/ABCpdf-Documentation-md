| Type | Default Value | Read Only | Description | 
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **[C#]** ```csharp bool ``` [Visual Basic] `Boolean` | false | No | Whether to provide native colors such as CMYK, separations and spot colors, or whether to convert all colors to RGB | 

## Notes
