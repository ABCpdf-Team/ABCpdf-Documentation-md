# EffectiveConformance Property

| **Type** | **Default** | **Read Only** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
|  | Pdf | Yes | The effective PdfConformance value. |

## Notes

This property indicates the effective conformance specified by the [Part](1-part.md) [Amd](2-amd.md) [Corr](3-corr.md), and [Conformance](4-conformance.md) properties.

It can take any of the following values:

* Pdf â€“ PDF conformance.
* PdfA1b - PDF/A Part 1 Level B conformance.
* PdfA1a - PDF/A Part 1 Level A conformance.
* PdfA2b - PDF/A Part 2 Level B conformance.
* PdfA2u - PDF/A Part 2 Level U conformance.
* PdfA2a - PDF/A Part 2 Level A conformance.
* PdfUA1 - PDF/UA Part 1 conformance.
