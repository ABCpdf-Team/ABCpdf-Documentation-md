# WritingMode Property

| **Type** | **Default** | **Read Only** | **Description** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| [C#] <BR> `WritingModeType` | n/a | Yes | Gets the font writing mode. |

## Notes

Gets the font writing mode.

The WritingModeType enum has two possible values:

* Horizontal
* Vertical
