| Type | Default | Read Only | Description | 
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Notes

The sub-type of annotation.

This may be 'Text', 'Link', 'Widget' or any of the many supported types listed in Section 8.4.5 of the [Adobe PDF Specification](http://partners.adobe.com/).
